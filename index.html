Vocabulary for Grade 1 Students & Parents

Module 7 詞彙指南

1. Deep

Going Down

Far down from the top.

深的：很長一段距離。

2. Divide

Sharing

To split into parts.

分開：分成幾部分。

3. Fiery

Hot!

Very hot and bright like fire.

熾熱的：像火一樣熱。

4. Fluffy

Soft Fur

Soft and light.

蓬鬆的：柔軟又輕。

5. Nature

The Forest

Trees, animals, and rivers.

大自然：森林與動物。

6. Break

Rest

休息時間

Snap

折斷

7. Direction

Which way?

The way you go.

方向：走哪條路。

8. Landed

Touch Ground

Coming down from the air.

著陸：落到地面。

9. Mission

Special Job

An important task.

任務：重要工作。

10. Problem

Puzzle

Something to fix or solve.

問題：需要解決的事。

11. Spectacular

Amazing!

Beautiful and exciting to see.

壯觀的：令人興奮的美景。

12. Spindly

Long & Thin

Very thin legs or arms.

細長的：長且細。

Question 1: Nature

How does a seed change?

Seed

種子

Tree

大樹

Question 2: Safety

How to stay safe in a storm?

  • Stay inside. 待在室內。
  • Stay away from windows. 遠離窗戶。

Question 3: Deserts

Hot & Dry

Deserts have very little rain.

Camels live here.

沙漠：又熱又乾。
